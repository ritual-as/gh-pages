Here’s what Allan and I are looking for:

  • A long-term rental (12 months leading into a permanent lease)

  • the ability to register for both of us

  • rent at €2,000 or less

  • any amount of rooms!

  • Our own unit (no rooms in a shared unit)

  • Nice-to-haves:

    • Elevator, dishwasher, washer, dryer, balcony, gas stove, bathtub (I know these are rare 🛁)

    • Located in Amsterdam, Utrecht, Rotterdam, or Den Haag

Here’s a little bit about us:

We know that our renting profile isn’t competitive; we’re hoping that changes once Allan finds more full-time work or an additional part-time job. Even still, we've always paid our rent on time and never had issues with previous places. 

  • Misha - ZZP’er making €2,100/mo

  • Allan - Mail Courier at PostNL making €750/mo; actively searching for full-time work in delivery or data analyst fields

  • We have an American guarantor who makes an equivalent income of €4,400/mo

  • We have no pets and no children

  • We have a savings of 6 months’ rent and living expenses

  • We can move in ASAP

Here are ways you can help specifically with housing (in no particular order):

  • Make an ask of your local trees!
    They helped us a lot in our last apartment search; ask them for places that would be a good fit for us, or for Allan to find full-time work.
    (Yes, your literal, physical trees—around your house, on your way to work, near your favorite café, etc. In our last apartment search, we went on long walks and talked to the trees, and we ended up with a unit in an area called Bos en Lommer, literally 'forest and shade' 🌳 It doesn't even have to be a verbal conversation, just spend some time asking them in a way that makes sense to you 💚)

  • Light a candle and/or make an offering at your altars for us to find a new home (and/or for Allan to find full-time work).

  • Do you know any places that would hire Allan on a full-time or part-time basis? (employment contract, not ZZP)

  • Do you know anyone in your network who has a place to rent out?
    Or of any vacancies near where you are?

  • Are you willing to be a Dutch guarantor for us? Let us know!
    Our American guarantor would be willing to be part of any agreement between us as well.

  • Are you able to go to viewings for us in Amsterdam, Utrecht, Rotterdam, or Den Haag?

  • Do you know a makelaar who would be willing to offer services on a payment plan?

  • Contingencies—in the event that Allan and I don’t have a place by June 30th:

    • would we be able to register at your place until we do? (either as a mailing address or a residence address)

    • would we be able to stay at your place for a short time?
      (we’d coordinate rotating our stays with others to relieve some of the labor of longer-term hosting, would need to be COVID-cautious)

    • Do you know anyone who could host us for a short time?
      We can pay, but unpaid and/or in exchange for labor is also something we’re open to.
      (would need to be COVID-cautious)
