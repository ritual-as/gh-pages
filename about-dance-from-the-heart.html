about Dance from the Heart

General format:
Landing & visioning - 10 min
Movement - 35-40 min
Reflection + closing - 10 min

In co-creating our Dance from the Heart spaces, participants describe experiencing a range of impacts, including:⁠
💆🏽 Decreased stress and anxiety⁠
♾️ Increased ability to resource, ground, and anchor ourselves in daily life⁠
🌿 Increased (re)connection to ancestral healing practices⁠
☀️ Increased confidence and self-efficacy⁠
🤝🏽 Increased connection, community, and belonging⁠

⁠Accessibility Details:⁠
🧘🏽‍♀️ No dance experience required! Participation can be from seated, standing, or reclined positions. There is no choreography, just improvised and intuitive movement and stillness.⁠
💻 Class is virtual, joining from a laptop or desktop computer is recommended
💬 This series will take place via Zoom with automated captions.⁠
🕶️ Freedom to be on/off camera and audio⁠
📵 This session will not be recorded.⁠
ASL interpretation currently not available.
