sliding scale

The sliding scale pricing I offer is informed by these considerations of a sliding scale for economic justice. Pricing for organizations is different than pricing for individuals, but can still be offered on a sliding scale. For company-sponsored pricing, please email ritual_as@disroot.org.

I'm also happy to work with individuals on no-interest, no-fee payment plans, as well as working with you to draft a plan for petitioning your job to cover my services as professional development. 

The basic sliding scale guidelines are:

  • Pay lower on the scale if you are not always able to get your basic needs met and have have little or no expendable income.
  • Pay around the middle of the scale if you stress about getting your basic needs met but are regularly able to do so, and if you have some expendable income.
  • Pay higher on the scale if you are comfortably able to get your basic needs met and regularly have expendable income.
